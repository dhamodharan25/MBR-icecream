About Us

MBR - Heaven of ice creams

MBR Ice Cream is a leading ice cream manufacturer based in the vibrant city of Coimbatore. Established in 2013, our company has been delighting customers with premium quality ice cream products for over 11 years. From classic flavors to innovative creations, we take pride in crafting indulgent treats that captivate taste buds and create memorable experiences.

Mission

At MBR Ice Cream, our mission is simple to spread joy, one scoop at a time. We are committed to producing the highest quality ice cream using the finest ingredients, innovative techniques, and a passion for flavor. Our goal is to exceed customer expectations by delivering delicious, satisfying treats that bring people together and create moments of happiness.

Vision

At our core, we envision a world where every scoop of our ice cream brings people together, creating moments of joy, connection, and indulgence. We strive to be more than just a dessert; we aim to become an integral part of communities, celebrated for our commitment to quality, creativity, and inclusivity. With each flavor, we aspire to ignite smiles, spark conversations, and build lasting memories.
